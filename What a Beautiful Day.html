Synopsis

What a Beautiful Day is a lovely short film about the struggles families have trying to connect with their loved ones, due to the unfortunate disease of Alzheimer’s. It is shown through a single moment between a mother and daughter one beautiful afternoon in a park. During a visit with her mother, Ashley struggles to connect with her mother, Mary. From the start of their visit, to just their general conversation. It shows how she can change for minute to minute. Ashley is further saddened by her mother’s responses and seeing that her memories are slowly fading away because of Alzheimer’s. By the end of their visit, there is a glimmer of hope for Ashley, she gets to spend a few moments of the afternoon with the mother she remembers.
