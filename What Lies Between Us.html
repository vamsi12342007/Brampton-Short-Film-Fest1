Synopsis

What Lies Between Us' is a dark and compelling drama that follows Anna, a young adult struggling with the aftermath of her mother Vanessa’s relentless battle with drug addiction.

Raised in the suburban Gold Coast, Australia, Anna is a bright and perceptive full-time university student, studying to become a psychologist.

Every day, she juggles her academic commitments while trying to support her mother. She reaches out to her father, Steve, for help, but he believes that Vanessa can only help herself.

As Anna’s life spirals out of control, she seeks guidance from her university teacher, Sue, who offers support and advice.

Anna discovers Vanessa’s hidden journal, exposing her private thoughts and secrets. As she reads through the entries, Anna uncovers painful truths about her parents’ troubled marriage and the hidden reasons behind Vanessa’s relapse. Later that night, tragedy strikes, Vanessa overdoses on heroin and passes away.

Determined to uncover the full truth, Anna confronts her father in a final attempt to understand his side of the story and the real reason behind her mother's tragic decline.
